About Us

More Than Just a Gym

Established in 2019, TRUE FITNESS is a fully equipped unisex gym located in Sector 115, Mohali. They boast a wide range of imported Viva Fitness machines, ensuring a comprehensive workout experience for their members. TRUE FITNESS offers personalized training programs including weight loss, weight gain, muscle building, and nutrition plans. Their certified trainers and supportive community atmosphere make TRUE FITNESS the perfect place to achieve your fitness goals.

Our expert coaches design personalized programs tailored to your goals — whether you want to lose weight, build muscle, or simply feel stronger and healthier every day.

Services Provided: Certified Personal Training, Weight Loss, Weight Gain, Muscle Building, Strength & Conditioning.
